# Introduction

To start from I am using this book hosted at https://d2l.ai/chapter_preface/index.html

Machine learning was not known that much back then as much it is known now.

Testing the potential of deep learning presents unique challenges because any single application brings together various disciplines. Applying deep learning requires simultaneously understanding.
i) the motivation for casting a problem in a particular way.
ii) The mathematical form of a given model
iii) The optimization algorithms for fitting the models to a data
iv) The statistical principles that tells us when we should expect our models to generalize unseen data and practical methods for certifying that they have in fact generalized.
v) the engineering techniques required to train models efficiently, navigating the pitfalls of numerical computing and getting the most of the available hardware.
